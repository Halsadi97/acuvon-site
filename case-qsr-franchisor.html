ZBB implementation for a major QSR franchisor

Zero Based Budgeting · June 20, 2020

Client Background

The client is a major QSR franchisor that set out to control its cost base and align spend with strategy through a zero-based budgeting programme.

Business Problem
  • High and rising G&A expenses with limited visibility into cost drivers
  • Spend not consistently aligned with strategic priorities
Solution Overview
  • Applied a Zero-Based Budgeting approach to rebuild the cost base from the ground up
  • Defined expense categories and standardized cost drivers through benchmarking
  • Identified and implemented initiatives to optimize costs and free capital for growth
